FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions.

1. What kind of websites do you build?

I build all types of websites — including personal portfolios, e-commerce stores, business websites, and custom web apps. Whether you need a simple landing page or a full-featured online platform, I can design and develop it from start to finish.

2. Do you offer both design and development?

Yes! I take care of both the front-end design (UI/UX) and back-end development. I make sure your website looks great, works fast, and functions exactly how you want.

3. How long does it take to complete a project?

It depends on the project size and features. A simple website can take 3–5 days, while larger web apps or e-commerce platforms may take 1–3 weeks. I’ll always give you a clear timeline before we start.

4. Can you make my website responsive and mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. Every website I build is fully responsive, which means it will look and work perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

5. How do we get started?

You can contact me through the form on my website or email me directly. Tell me about your idea or what kind of website you need, and I’ll get back to you with a plan, timeline, and quote.
